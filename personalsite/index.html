Hello There!

My name is Rebecca Hanks and I am a classically trained violinist who has been playing violin for over 17 years.

I have apprenticed before beginning my teaching career and I am now propelling children and adults alike through their violin training with the NYC Violin Studio Beginner Method as well as Suzuki Method!

I truly love to work with children, and it's a to work with adults. I never let my students feel rushed or held back by the teaching pace. I deliver lessons online and in person at my home.

No matter where you are, I will make your beginner violin lessons are a relaxed and stress free experience and will help bring the joy of music into your life!

I deliver violin lessons to Beginner and Early Intermediate violin students of all ages.
